What we do

Our Services

From concept to completion, we handle every aspect of your kitchen or bathroom transformation.

Kitchen Fitting

We supply and install bespoke kitchens to your exact specification. From initial design through to final fitting, we handle cabinets, worktops, appliances, splashbacks and all associated plumbing and electrical work. Every kitchen is unique — we work with your space, your style and your budget.

Bathroom Renovation

Full bathroom transformations including suite selection and installation, tiling, plumbing, electrical, plastering and finishing. Whether you want a simple refresh or a complete luxury redesign, we coordinate every trade so you don't have to.

Tiling & Flooring

Precision tiling in ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, mosaic and feature tiles. Floor and wall tiling for kitchens, bathrooms, hallways and living spaces. Large format tiles, herringbone patterns, brickbond layouts — we do it all with meticulous attention to detail.

Joinery

Bespoke joinery crafted to your specification. Fitted wardrobes, alcove shelving, media walls, under-stair storage, window boards and custom carpentry. We design and build pieces that are built to last and perfectly integrated into your home.
